Frequently Asked Questions

How long do window wraps last?
Our window wraps are made with premium vinyl materials rated for 5–7 years of outdoor use. With proper care, many last even longer. All installations come with our full warranty.
Can I still see out through the window wraps?
Yes! Our perforated window vinyl is designed for one-way visibility. From the outside, people see your full-color graphics. From the inside, you can see through clearly. Natural light still comes in.
How long does the process take?
Most projects go from approved design to completed installation in 5–7 business days. Rush options are available if you need it sooner. We’ll give you a timeline with your quote.
Do you handle the design?
We handle everything. Our design team will create a custom mockup based on your brand, vision, and space. If you have existing artwork or brand guidelines, we’ll work from those. Design consultation is included.
Will the wraps damage my windows?
No. Our vinyl is specifically designed for commercial glass and removes cleanly without residue or damage. Perfect for leased spaces or seasonal promotions.
How much do window wraps cost?
Pricing depends on the size of your windows, type of material, and complexity of the design. Most storefront projects range from $1,000 to $3,000. The best way to get an accurate price is to request your free same-day quote.
Do you serve businesses outside of Rancho Cucamonga?
Absolutely! We regularly serve businesses throughout Ontario, Upland, Fontana, Claremont, Pomona, and the greater Inland Empire. Contact us to confirm we cover your area.
What types of businesses benefit from window wraps?
Any business with street-facing windows! We’ve done wraps for retail stores, restaurants, dental offices, salons, gyms, law firms, real estate offices, medical clinics, and more.
