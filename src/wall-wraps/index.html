Frequently Asked Questions

How long do wall wraps last?
Indoor wall wraps installed on properly prepped surfaces typically last 7–10 years without fading, peeling, or yellowing. Most customers replace them when they want to refresh the design, not because the material has failed.
Will the wrap damage my paint or drywall?
Not when installed correctly. We use removable wall vinyl on most projects, which lifts cleanly without pulling paint or leaving residue. For leased spaces, we always recommend the removable option so you can take it down without a deposit headache.
Can you wrap textured walls?
Yes — we work with textured drywall, light orange-peel, and even some heavier textures using specialty conformable vinyls. For very rough surfaces (heavy stucco, brick, cinder block), we’ll recommend an alternate approach during your quote so you know exactly what to expect.
Do you handle the design, or do I need my own artwork?
We handle everything. Our design team will create a custom mockup based on your brand, vision, and the photos you send us of the actual wall. If you have existing artwork or brand guidelines, we’ll work from those. Design consultation is included.
How long does the whole process take?
Most wall wrap projects go from approved design to completed install in 7–10 business days. Larger or more complex installs may take longer. Rush options are available — just ask when you request your quote.
How much does a wall wrap cost?
Pricing depends on wall size, surface prep, material, and design complexity. Most accent walls run $800–$2,500, and full-wall murals or wraparound installs typically range from $2,500–$8,000+. The best way to get an accurate price is to request your free same-day quote.
Will the install disrupt my business?
Most wall installs are completed in a single day with minimal disruption. We can schedule installs after hours, on weekends, or during off-peak times for retail and office environments. Just tell us your scheduling needs when you request your quote.
Do you serve businesses outside of Rancho Cucamonga?
Absolutely. We regularly install wall wraps throughout Ontario, Upland, Fontana, Claremont, Pomona, Chino, and the greater Inland Empire — and for larger projects, we’ve traveled across Southern California. Contact us to confirm we cover your area.
